Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What materials do you use?

We primarily use PLA+ and PETG for durability, smooth texture, and clean finishes.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 2–4 business days. Custom prints may take longer.

Do you accept custom print requests?

Yes! Email custom@ff.inc with your idea and we’ll work with you to make it real.

Are your fidgets durable?

All items are printed with reinforced walls and high‑quality filament for long‑term use.
